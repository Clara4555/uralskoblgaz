SUSTAINABILITY

Uralskoblgaz Refinery LLP is one of the leading modern enterprises in the oil refining industry of Kazakhstan.

Strategic planning and implementation of environmental protection measures is the main postulate of development and aspiration aimed at ensuring a high level of environmental safety.

Uralskoblgaz Refinery LLP is guided by the following strategic goals in the field of environmental protection:

  • Reducing the negative impact on the environment.
  • Resource saving, increasing energy efficiency of production processes, and reducing greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Increasing environmental competence and employee involvement to reduce risks and improve environmental management.

Uralskoblgaz Refinery LLP, in accordance with the requirements of the Environmental Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan, ensures production environmental control.

The goals of industrial environmental control are:

  • Obtaining information for decision-making regarding internal environmental policy and regulation of production processes.
  • Ensuring compliance with environmental legislation.
  • Minimizing the negative impact of production on the environment and human health.
  • Increasing the efficiency of natural and energy resource usage.
  • Prompt proactive response to emergency situations.
  • Raising environmental awareness among employees.
  • Informing the public about environmental activities.
  • Enhancing the environmental management system's efficiency.

Industrial environmental control is carried out under the Industrial Environmental Control Program, which is part of the environmental impact permit.

Industrial environmental control is carried out on the basis of the Industrial Environmental Control Program, which is part of the environmental impact permit. Environmental assessment of the efficiency of the production process within the framework of industrial environmental control is carried out on the basis of measurements and (or) calculations of the level of emissions into the environment, harmful production factors, as well as the actual volume of consumption of natural, energy and other resources. One of the main elements of the Industrial Environmental Control Program is industrial monitoring of environmental objects.
