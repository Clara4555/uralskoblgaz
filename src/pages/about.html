ABOUT US

Our Background

Our vision is to be the premier provider of petroleum products and value-added services in the industry. We strive to achieve this by consistently meeting or exceeding our customers’ expectations and by continuously improving our operations.

Our mission is to safely and responsibly produce high-quality petroleum products that meet or exceed industry standards, while providing exceptional customer service and value-added services.

Uralskoblgaz Refinery Group is a leading provider of petroleum products and services in the industry. We have been in business for over 20 years and have built a reputation for quality, safety, and reliability.

We have grown to become one of the largest refining companies in the region, with several facilities located across the country. Our experienced team of professionals is dedicated to meeting the needs of our customers and providing them with the best solutions and services in the industry.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by a team of experienced professionals, allowing us to offer a wide range of high quality services.

As an industry leader, we know the value of delivering top quality products across the world. We are committed to delivering high-quality petroleum products and services to our customers, so that they can run their businesses smoothly.

Our products are of the highest quality and are compliant with industry standards. Our quality assurance processes ensure that our products meet the specifications required by our customers. We also offer customized blending solutions to meet the specific needs of our customers. We understand that each customer has unique requirements and we work closely with them to develop tailored solutions that meet their needs.

The Pride of the Kazakhstan's Oil Industry

Uralskoblgaz Refinery is the most trusted fuel company in Kazakhstan, with a refining capacity of more than 27 million m³ of crude oil every year. We refine and sell petroleum products such as gasoline, diesel, heating oil, renewable fuels, and many others to companies and consumers in Kazakhstan and abroad.

We are specialized in producing high-quality petroleum products for a variety of industries. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by a team of experienced professionals. We take pride in our commitment to safety, environmental responsibility, and efficiency.

What We Do

From Raw Material to Customer

We conduct extensive refinement of crude oil and the sale of petroleum products to oil companies active in Kazakhstan and on the international market.

We also offer a variety of services to support our customers to meet their specific needs. These include sales of petroleum products, oil & gas processing, logistics & transportation solutions, technical support, and laboratory testing services. Our team of experts is available to assist you every step of the way.
